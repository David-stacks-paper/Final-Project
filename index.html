Columbus, Ohio

Everything I've learned growing up in the capital city.

Why stay here?

I've lived in Columbus my whole life. I've only left Ohio once, and honestly? I don't need to go anywhere else. This city has grown up with me. From the way downtown has transformed into a real food scene to the fact that we have the best parks, it feels like home in a way nowhere else could. It's affordable, it's friendly, and there is always something new to find in the neighborhoods.

10 Must-Visit Places

Scioto Mile

My go-to spot for a walk; the skyline view never gets old.

Columbus Zoo

An absolute classic, been going here since I was a kid.

Short North

The place to be for art, galleries, and just people-watching.

COSI

It's science, but even as an adult, it's still fun to visit.

Franklin Park

The conservatory glass house is beautiful year-round.

Ohio Stadium

You haven't really experienced Columbus until you've heard a game here.

Easton

It’s huge, it’s always busy, and it has every store you'd need.

North Market

This is where I go when I want the best local food.

Goodale Park

My favorite quiet place to sit and look at the trees.

German Village

The brick streets and houses are the most charming part of the city.
